Seth Gabel | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Seth Gabel is a well-known American actor. He is most known for playing agent Lincoln Lee on Fox’s Fringe and Cotton Mather on WGN America’s Salem.

He has also appeared in several notable films, including Tadpole, The Da Vinci Code, and Jonah Hex.

Where was Seth Gabel born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education
