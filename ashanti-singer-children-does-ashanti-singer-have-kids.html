Ashanti (singer) Children: Does Ashanti (singer) have Kids?

Ashanti is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and record producer. As of this writing, Ashanti does not have any kids.

She rose to fame in the early 2000s as a prominent figure in the R&B and hip-hop music scene. She began her career as a background vocalist and songwriter, working with artists like Jennifer Lopez and Big Pun.

However, she gained widespread recognition when she signed with Murder Inc. Records. Her debut self-titled album, “Ashanti,” released in 2002, was a massive success and established her as a solo artist.

The album featured hit singles like “Foolish” and “Happy” and earned her a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary R&B Album.

Ashanti’s music career continued to thrive with subsequent albums, including “Chapter II” (2003), “Concrete Rose” (2004), and others.

Aside from her music career, Ashanti has ventured into acting, appearing in films and TV series. She has also continued to release new music and collaborate with other artists, making her a respected and influential figure in the entertainment industry.
