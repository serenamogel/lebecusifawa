Tyla - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media star and singer who is best known for her tyla_ TikTok account where she posts comedy, lip-sync, and singing videos. She has accumulated over 6 million followers on the platform. She has gained over 2.3 million followers on Instagram where she posts modeling content.

Before Fame

She launched her TikTok account in March 2020. 

Trivia

She and her sister have a YouTube channel called Sydney and Tyla. A popular TikTok from December of 2021 features her dancing in front of a moonlight sky. It has earned over 3 million views. She released the viral hit song "Water" in August of 2023.

Family Life

Her nephew and cousin have appeared on her TikTok account. She has an older sister named Whitney, a younger brother named Tyrese, and a younger sister named Sydney.

Associated With

She posted a duet singing the Elvis Presley song "Can't Help Falling In Love" in August 2020.
