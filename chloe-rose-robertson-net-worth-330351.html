About

Actress and singer best known for playing the role of Luna in the Paramount Plus series Wolf Pack. She also played Esther in the film Wildflower. She shares behind-the-scenes photos and clips from the set of Wolf Pack on her chloeroserobertson TikTok account, which has earned 50,000 followers.

She first started out in musical theater but her love for performing on-screen quickly led her to set her sights on roles in film and television. She is also a singer and enjoys writing both screenplays and music.

She has released two singles, including "Empty" and "You Can't Be My Man." She was featured on an episode of Entertainment Tonight in 2023.

She is the daughter of Michelle and Steve Robertson. She has one brother named Cole.

She appears opposite Bella Shepard and Armani Jackson in Wolf Pack.
