Early Life & Jimmie Allen’s Net Worth

Jimmie Allen was born on June 18, 1986 in Milton, Delaware (United States). In 2007, Jimmie Allen later moved to Nashville, Tennessee when he was 21 years old. During the early years of his life in Nashville, Jimmie experienced extreme poverty and had to live out of his car. Then, he receive the opportunity of the lifetime – auditioning for America’s Got Talent in Nashville.

Unfortunately, Jimmie was unable to pass the initial rounds of America’s Got Talent.

Jimmie Allen, then, made an attempt to audition for American Idol, unfortunately, failing that as well.

Though he did not pass the early rounds of American Idol nor America’s Got Talent, he was able to persist and sign a publishing deal with Wide Open Music. Later, he was signed to Broken Bow Records in 2017.

Jimmie Allen Career & Net Worth

Jimmie Allen’s music career started after he toured together with American Idol’s winner Scotty McCreery. In 2016, he signed a deal with Wide Open Music. As mentioned, he was signed to Broken Bow Records and released his self-titled debut EP.

In 2020, he was nominated to be the New Artist of the Year. Jimmie Allen also performed for the CMA Awards Show.

One of the tracks from his EP “Blue Jean Baby” was a massive hit and was added to Spotify’s “United States Viral 50 chart”. In 2018, he released the single “Best Shot”. Within a week of its release, it was a second most-added song to country music radio playlists. The song managed to chart in the Hot Country Songs, Country Airplay, and Billboard Hot 100 charts.

In October 2018, Jimmie released his debut album titled “Mercury Lane” which was produced by Ash Bowers. The song “Best Shot” from that album was a number one hit on the Billboard Country Airplay chart in November 2018. The album’s second single “Make Me Want To” also became Number 1 hit on the Billboard Country Airplay chart.
