More Stellas Ice Cream shops opening in the Treasure Valley

Boise, Idaho (CBS2) – Stella’s Ice Cream is expanding across Idaho. Three new locations will be built in Boise, Meridian and Kuna.

The Boise location will open at 4740 W State Street in Boise. That’s the same location as the old Baskin Robbins store.

The exact locations in Meridian and Kuna are still being decided.

Stella’s is also opening a location in Twin Falls this weekend. It will have a full espresso drive-thru option.
