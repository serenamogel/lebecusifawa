What is neoderm cream used for

Can Neoderm cream be used on face?

Neoderm Plus Cream should not be used on the face. Do not use the medication for longer periods of time than you have been advised. Do not apply a bandage or dressing to the area being treated, as this will increase absorption of the preparation and increase the risk of side-effects.

Is Dermasone a organic compound?

This medication is a mild corticosteroid.

What is Triderm cream used for?

This medication is used to treat a variety of skin conditions (e.g., eczema, dermatitis, allergies, rash). Triamcinolone reduces the swelling, itching, and redness that can occur in these types of conditions. This medication is a medium- to strong-potency corticosteroid.

Is Fucicort antifungal?

Pharmacodynamics: Fucicort Cream combines the potent topical antibacterial action of fusidic acid with the anti-inflammatory and antipruritic effects of betamethasone valerate.

Where should you not use hydrocortisone cream?

Only use hydrocortisone skin treatments on children under 10 years old if a doctor recommends it. Creams you can buy are not supposed to be used on the eyes, around the bottom or genitals, or on broken or infected skin.

Can you use Triderm on lady parts?

If you are using this medication to treat symptoms only in and around the lady parts, products applied directly inside the lady parts should be considered before medications that are taken by mouth, absorbed through the skin, or injected.

Is Triderm good for wounds?

This medication is used to prevent and treat minor skin infections caused by small cuts, scrapes, or burns. It is available without a prescription for self-medication.Do not use this product over large areas of the body.
