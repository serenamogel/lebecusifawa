Marcell Ozuna Parents: Meet Marcelino Ozuna And Adriana Idelfonso

Marcell Ozuna is a Dominican professional baseball outfielder for the Atlanta Braves of Major League Baseball.

Marcell Ozuna Parents: Meet Marcelino Ozuna And Adriana Idelfonso

Marcell Ozuna’s parents are Marcelino Ozuna and Adriana Idelfonso.

Marcell’s father Marcelino Ozuna is a former painter, while his mother Adriana Idelfonso is a housekeeper.

Marcelino Ozuna and his wife Adriana Idelfonso share four children; Marcell Ozuna and three others whose details are not documented.

Their son Marcell Ozuna Idelfonso is a Dominican professional baseball player.

He made his MLB debut in 2013 with the Florida Marlins.

On December 14, 2017, the Marlins traded Ozuna to the St. Louis Cardinals and the Cardinals signed a one-year, $9 million contract with him on January 12, 2018.

In January 2020, Ozuna signed a one-year contract worth $18 million with the Atlanta Braves.

On February 5, 2021, Ozuna re-signed with the Braves with a four-year, $64million, deal.

Marcell Ozuna is a two-time MLB All-Star (2016, 2017), and won both a Gold Glove and a Silver Slugger Award in 2017.
