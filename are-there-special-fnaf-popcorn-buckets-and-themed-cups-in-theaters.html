Are there special FNAF popcorn buckets and themed cups in theaters?

Still hanging on to that limited edition Jigsaw-shaped popcorn bucket from the premiere of Saw X? Or how about Ghostface-inspired snack collectible from when Scream VI was slashing into theaters earlier this year?

Recommended Videos

Either way, chances are both eye-popping items are prized possessions which are perfectly propped up in some part of your home where all can see it. Of course, I wouldn’t blame you, seeing as heading to the theaters and nabbing a limited edition collectible is evidence of a golden viewing experience.

Aside from instances such as Transformers and Taylor Swift’s Eras tour movie, a handful of modern-day horror movies have incorporated the inclusion of themed popcorn buckets alongside these theatrical releases. So with Blumhouse’s live-action Five Nights at Freddy’s set to hit theaters at the end of this week, it’s only fitting that horror fanatics and die-hard supporters of the iconic video game series are wondering if any FNAF-themed popcorn buckets will be made available when the film hits theaters and Peacock in a few short days.

Are there any FNAF popcorn buckets or themed cups?

Unfortunately, for those who prefer to fully indulge in the charming movie experience by wrapping their fingers around a specialized popcorn bucket, there won’t be any personalized FNAF popcorn buckets or themed cups to be enjoyed throughout the movie. This is a major letdown, of course, seeing as the supernatural narrative has attracted a wide range of Freddy Fazbear fanatics over the years, but those same folks will sadly have to settle for a regular popcorn container for the movie.

As far as a silver lining goes, however, it has been reported that select Regal Cinema locations will be offering a FNAF-inspired pizza ,box which replicates items from Freddy Fazbear’s infamous pizzeria. So while the special popcorn buckets might be out, eat least you can scarf down a delicious pizza while watching Freddy and the gang terrorize others.
