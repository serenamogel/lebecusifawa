Niecy Nash With Partner Jessica Betts: The First Queer Couple On Essence Mag Cover

Niecy Nash made history with her partner, Jessica Betts, as they became the first same-sex couple to be on the cover of Essence. They graced the cover of Essence's March/April 2022 issue, and both of them celebrated the issue via their Instagram posts.

Niecy Nash posted a picture from the shoot and wrote, "Making HERSTORY. Thank you @essence for choosing The Betts as your FIRST same sex couple to cover you magazine! #BettsOfWords #MakingHistory #Herstory #BlackHistoryMonth" She also posted a photo of them posing topless and wrote, "Simpley The Betts L o v e > U N C O V E R E D."

Niecy's partner, Jessica, also shared the cover on her Instagram and wrote, "H E R S T O R Y Thank you @essence for allowing Love to win! First #SameSex couple to Ever grace the Cover." Moreover, the couple opened up about their love story and shared their interests with each other.

Nash explained to the magazine that she was attracted most to Jessica's Soul as she had the most beautiful soul she had ever met. She also said that she couldn't imagine going through life now that she had experienced it. Nash also talked about how challenging and compounded she felt when she realized she had feelings for a woman who was her friend. 

The pair also discussed their marriage announcement and said that they held hands and prayed before uploading the post. They explained how they were shocked to have found the internet broke from their marriage announcement. However, the couple never shied away from displaying their PDA, and Nash shared that they were extremely compatible. She explained that she felt like the love that she received from Bett was custom as opposed to off the rack and felt like the assignment happened in the spirit realm before they ever knew about it.

Niecy Nash Married Life With Partner Jessica Betts

Niecy Nash and Jessica Betts tied the knot in August 2020. Niecy posted a picture of their wedding ceremony on both Instagram and Twitter and captioned the photo, "Mrs. Carol Denise Betts #Lovewins."

Net Worth Of Niecy Nash

Niecy Nash has an estimated net worth of $4 million as an American actor, comedian, and presenter. Her partner, Jessica Betts net worth is around $200k as of 2020.
