Anhelina Kalinina Nationality: Where is tennis player Kalinina from?

Anhelina Serhiyivna Kalinina is a tennis player from Ukraine. She has won 15 singles titles and three doubles titles on the ITF Circuit.

She earned her career-high singles ranking of world No. 35 on June 6, 2022. She reached No. 196 in the WTA doubles rankings on July 15, 2019. She is now the best tennis player in Ukraine.

Kalinina made her Grand Slam singles main-draw debut at the 2018 US Open after winning three qualifying matches.

She defeated Elena-Gabriela Ruse in the first qualifying round, Tereza Martincová in round two, and Jaimee Fourlis in round three, all in straight sets, and went on to win a Grand Slam match by defeating Kathinka von Deichmann in three sets before losing in the second round to Sloane Stephens.

Despite losing in the final round of Wimbledon qualifying, Kalinina went on to win the $60k Montpellier and $100k Contrexéville trophies over the next two weeks, bringing her 2021 ITF championship collection to four.
