Euphoria Actor Angus Cloud Dies at Age 25

In devastating news, actor Angus Cloud died on Monday. The Euphoria star was 25 years old.

Cloud’s family released a statement to TMZ, saying, “It is with the heaviest heart that we had to say goodbye to an incredible human today. As an artist, a friend, a brother and a son, Angus was special to all of us in so many ways.”

While what happened to Angus isn’t publicly known, his family said, “Last week he buried his father and intensely struggled with this loss. The only comfort we have is knowing Angus is now reunited with his dad, who was his best friend. Angus was open about his battle with mental health, and we hope that his passing can be a reminder to others that they are not alone and should not fight this on their own in silence.”

“We hope the world remembers him for his humor, laughter, and love for everyone. We ask for privacy at this time as we are still processing this devastating loss.”

TMZ later reported a 911 call made around 11:30 a.m. Monday by Cloud’s mother suggested a “possible overdose,” but police haven’t released a cause of death and are investigating.

Getty Images

Angus Cloud stands in front of Euphoria colleagues Hunter Schafer, Sam Levison, and Zendaya in January 2022.

Cloud is best known for playing Fezco on the HBO drama series Euphoria, which also stars Zendaya, Maude Apatow, and Sydney Sweeney. He also appeared in The Line, North Hollywood, and multiple music videos for artists like Karol G, Juice WRLD, and Becky G.

Variety reported that shortly before his death, Cloud completed production for his role in an untitled 2024 monster thriller film for Universal. He also finished filming Your Lucky Day, a horror thriller whose release date hasn’t been announced.
